# free time

What is a person to do with "*free time*"? I finally cleared out my reading list and haven't had any perplexing ideas as of late. The particular books were *Designing Data-Intensive Systems*, a book on *Kubernetes*, a book on *Docker*, and *Post Growth: Life After Capitalism*.

I've operated the past month or so in a continual flood of things I wanted to deep-dive. The water has since retreated which has left me wondering what I should do. Of course I have class work that I have procrastinated on. So there's that. I also have full confidence in myself eventually getting distracted in the near future so the best prescription is therefore to breathe & enjoy the silence.
