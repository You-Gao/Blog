## Characters and Stands

I think everyone can agree. The creativity of characters and stands in Jojo's is what makes the manga series (atleast for me). I definitely am not discounting the other things Araki does very well in Jojo's like narrative, storytelling, themes, symbolism, and plot development. But, I find myself being hooked to the story 100% for the fights and interactions between the unique stand abilities. The strategic depth and variety in these fights add a layer of excitement and engagement, making each encounter feel fresh and unpredictable.Â  And it doesn't seem to get stale because each time Araki comes up with the perfect battleground for the new stand to battle it out.
