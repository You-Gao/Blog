# mspaint

I love "*mspaint*". *Microsoft* has not made a better app for the Windows ecosystem than *mspaint*. One could argue on whether *Task Manager* or *Notepad* is 2nd, but neither is close to the great *mspaint*. The quick load time and expressive capability of *mspaint* cements it in the Windows hall of fame.

## runner-ups

It would be redundant to write a blog praising *mspaint*, so here are some other useful apps I use.

### microsoft

* *notepad*: Simple, fast, and easily managed notes.
* *snip & sketch*: Always had to use Gyazo for this, but now they have a "*native*" solution.

### not-microsoft

* *windirstat*: Useful when cleaning up files to make space.
* *autohotkey*: A scripting language designed to make hotkeys.
